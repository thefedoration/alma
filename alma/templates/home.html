Inspire A Culture of Giving

Almify’s goal is to provide educational institutions with an interactive platform that allows for current students and recent graduates to make small donations on a rolling basis.

Easily Collect Information

Keeping track of graduates’ contact info can be challenging. Almify allows users sign up easily online or via mobile app and provides institutions with up-to-date contact details of all users.

Seamlessly Integrate with Current Systems

Almify easily integrates with existing donation-based infrastructure making internal tracking and reporting on donations effortless for your Advancement Team.

Easily Onboard New Users

Almify makes giving easy with a very simple onboarding process for students as well as institutions.

Create Personalized Class Portals

Each graduating class has its own “giving portal” allowing them to easily set and track class donation goals. The portal also serves as an instrumental medium by which institutions can message back to students and alumni.

Unlock "Almify Donations"

Almify’s goal is to help institutions inspire a culture of giving, and we’re willing to back that up with matching financial donations. Generous students, parents, alumni and faculty members can pose similar challenges to graduating classes, offering to match donations and inspiring students to reach and surpass class goals.

Your alumni will love Almify mobile

By providing it’s users with a simple, interactive, easy-to-use platform, Almify mobile makes it easier to engage, and easier to give

  • Clean mobile interface
  • One tap payment
  • Push notifications enabled
  • Almify dollars available when you join with your institution’s code
